    About High Quality

    Residential and Commercial Development Quality and Safety are our priorities. We are a highly experienced family-owned business operating in Edmonton. We have been providing a wide range of professional excavation and concrete services throughout the Edmonton and surrounding area. High Quality Excavation Inc. has the proper equipment and experience to efficiently and safely complete projects big or small.High Quality Excavation Inc. provides a wide and comprehensive range of services from residential basement excavation to concrete replacement. Some of the services provided are basement digging and backfilling, rough and final grading, concrete removal, concrete pouring and repairing. No job is too big or too small for us!
